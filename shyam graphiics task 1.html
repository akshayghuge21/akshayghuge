A testimonial is a personal statement that highlights a product’s, service’s, or individual’s key qualities and abilities. It’s a way to advocate for a business and its products or services in a specific, detailed way. Think of a testimonial as the most detailed review you can write. Call to Action

A Call to Action (CTA) on a website is an instruction to the visitor in order to encourage them to take some kind of action. A Call to Action might be simple instructional text, such as “call us now”, “find out more”, or “subscribe to our Newsletter”.
